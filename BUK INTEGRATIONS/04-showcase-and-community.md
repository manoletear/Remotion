## About Remotion

**The programmatic video dream**

Started as a side project in 2021, Remotion is now a company in Zurich, Switzerland, pushing to combine the powers of video editing and programming.

### Team

## Showcase - Projects Built with Remotion

Source: https://www.remotion.dev/showcase

### Featured Projects

**Next.js - Video tutorial**
Delba Oliveira visually explains Vercel's Next.js and the concept of React.

**Submagic - Viral shorts**
Make your short-form videos more captivating with Captions, B-Rolls, Zooms and Sound Effects.

**Revid - Storytelling social media videos**
Revid allows creators to turn ideas into social media videos in minutes. AI-powered platform to generate short-form storytelling. Remotion is used to preview and render videos.

**GitHub Unwrapped - Campaign**
A year-in-review video campaign for GitHub, where each user gets a personalized video based on their GitHub activity.

**Fluidmotion - Animated video backgrounds**
Create beautiful animated backgrounds for apps, videos or presentations.

**HeyGen - Video Agent**
Fully made with HeyGen's Video Agent, powered by Remotion. Avatar, script, motion graphics, and final edits created just by prompting.

**Banger Show - Music visuals**
High quality music visuals without learning 3D or video editing software.

**Vibrantsnap - Fast screenrecording**
Create product demos, training videos, and YouTube tutorials with auto layouts, captions, intelligent zoom, and 4K export.

**MUX - Visualize video stats**
Showcase your video stats by creating a dynamic animated video using Remotion and the Mux Data API.

**Hello Meteo - Weather app**
A weather report generator that gets OpenWeather data and renders a video with the forecast. Creates social media stories.

**Electricity Maps - Data visualization**
Visualization of heavy electricity data in Europe, showing fluctuations in grid emissions on a map.

**Watercolor Map - Animated map**
A travel animation showing journey on a map with watercolor effects. Perfect for b-roll footage.

**Relay.app - Automation tool**
Used Remotion to create dynamic, programmatically generated instructional videos keeping content consistent with brand standards.

**AnimStats - Animated statistics**
Transform statistics into captivating animated GIFs and Videos within minutes.

**MyKaraoke - Karaoke video maker**
Creates karaoke and lyric videos with AI-powered vocal removal and automatic lyric syncing.

**AdmoveAI - Ecommerce ads**
Automated advertising platform for eCommerce campaigns. Remotion builds and renders the videos.

**Hackreels - Animate your code**
Create beautiful code animations in seconds. Detects code changes automatically and makes stunning animations.

**SuperMotion - Product Videos**
Create engaging product promo videos from screen recordings or screenshots. Uses Remotion for cloud rendering.

**Remotion Recorder - Screencast videos**
Fully featured screen recording software built with Remotion. Record screen, webcam, and audio.
